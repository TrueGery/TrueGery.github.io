Project 1: DTIS Online

Brand and identity development is an essential process for shaping how a company is perceived by its target audience. In DTIS Online's case, I was involved as a project manager and marketing coordinator for it's development in an international market such as the one in the United States.

Initial Approach

Our client was struggling, they had no sales, they didn't know what to do, they were losing sales and customers. Later I met DTIS Online's owner, he was very nice and attentive, he said I was right, that diesel is a very nasty, that if we do want to use, we must burned it, as cleanly, and as efficiently as possible, and this cannot be done unless its diesel injectors are in perfect condition. Step 1: Gathering information

  1. Market research: competitors, diesel concept, potential clients.

  2. Targeting the audience: What they do, what they consume, which states do they live, what are their ages, do they have trucks? are they diesel technitians? are they diesel truckers? are they employees? are they diesel truck owners?

Step 2: Marketing Basic Concepts

  1. Brand identity: create a concept to develop a logo, colors, motto, etc.

  2. Graphic identity: develop a graphic identity in different visual marketing materials for: social media, website, banners (digital and physical), printing materials, etc.
